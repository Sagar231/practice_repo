KASHMIR : THE HEAVEN ON EARTH

At a distance of 250 kms from katra, lies the heaven on earth…….Kashmir. its not exactly in neighborhood but it does worth a visit if you ever visit Katra. Kashmir was the heaven for Jahangir. His famous words, “Gar firdaus, ruhe zamin ast, hamin asto, hamin asto, hamin ast” (If there is ever a heaven on earth, it’s here, it’s here, it’s here), are now the tourism signature line for the State! Kashmir is dotted with beautiful lakes, surrounded by magnificent valleys. And it is bestowed with nature’s bounty in terms of fruits and flowers. September is the season for the Apple and Apricot orchards to bloom. Srinagar is a city with a heart-wrenching past and present. To understand India, it is important to understand Kashmir. Surprisingly, most Indians don’t, let alone the world.
